Gojira v1.3 (September 2020), by James Pino

Gojira helps open and create multiple Jira tickets quickly and easily without having to wait for a page to load! To get Gojira working for your project, click the configure button and enter your Jira's base URL and a comma-separated list of project prefixes. That's it!

Here's how it works:

Open a single ticket:

ABC-123

Open multiple tickets of the samne project:

ABC-123/212/198

Open a ticket for several different projects:

ABC-123,XYZ-212,FOO-999

Open multiple tickets for multiple projects:

ABC-123/212/198,XYZ-212,FOO-111/999
